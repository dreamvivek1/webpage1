Sidharth Shukla

Sidharth Shukla (born 12 December 1980)[2] is an Indian actor,Historic winner of BiggBoss 13, model and television host who primarily works in Hindi television. He started his career as a model.He won the World's Best Model title in November 2005, beating 40 other participants from across Asia, Latin America, and Europe.
